The Navier-Stokes equations for two-phase flow are given as follows, 
\begin{equation}
	\frac{\partial \rho\bf{u}}{{\partial t}}+\nabla\cdot({\rho \bf{u}\bf{u}})=-\nabla p+\mu\nabla^2\bf{u}+{\rho\bf{g}}
\end{equation}
